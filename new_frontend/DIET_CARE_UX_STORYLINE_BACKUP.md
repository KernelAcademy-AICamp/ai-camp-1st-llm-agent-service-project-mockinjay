**Demographics:**
- Age: 58 years old
- Gender: Male
- Occupation: Office worker (partially retired)
- CKD Stage: Stage 3 (eGFR 45 mL/min)
- Tech Literacy: Moderate (uses smartphone, basic apps)

**Health Profile:**
- Diagnosed with CKD 2 years ago
- Comorbidities: Type 2 diabetes, hypertension
- Current treatment: Medication, dietary management
- Dietary restrictions: Low sodium (2000mg/day), controlled protein (0.8g/kg), low potassium (2000mg/day)

**Goals & Motivations:**
- Prevent disease progression to dialysis
- Manage daily nutrition without feeling overwhelmed
- Understand which foods are safe to eat
- Track progress and feel in control

- Confused about what foods to eat (conflicting information online)

- Feels discouraged when making mistakes
- Struggles to calculate sodium/potassium content

- Smartphone: Samsung Galaxy (Android)
- Usage time: Morning (7-9am), Lunch (12-1pm), Evening (6-8pm)
- Prefers simple, clear interfaces
- Needs larger text, high contrast
- Values voice input and image-based features
